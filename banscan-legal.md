Privacy Policy for BanScan

Effective Date: June 25, 2026

BanScan helps users screen product ingredient labels against banned-substance lists.

Information We Collect

BanScan may collect or process the following information when you use the app:

Photos or images you choose to scan or upload
Text extracted from product ingredient labels
Ingredient search or scan inputs you submit in the app
Basic technical information needed to operate the app
How We Use Information

We use this information to:

Extract ingredient text from product labels
Check ingredients against banned-substance lists
Display potential matches and supporting substance details
Maintain and improve app functionality
Third-Party Services

BanScan may send product label images and extracted text to OpenAI to support ingredient extraction and analysis. OpenAI processes this information according to its applicable terms and privacy practices.

We do not sell user data. We do not use user photos, ingredient text, or scan data for advertising or cross-app tracking.

Data Retention

BanScan does not require users to create an account. BanScan is designed to process scans for app functionality and does not intentionally store user scan history on our servers.

Third-party services may retain submitted data according to their own policies and service terms.

User Choices

Users can choose whether to grant camera or photo library access. Users can avoid uploading a photo by using direct search where available.
